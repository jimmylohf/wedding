Getting There

By Car

The main public entrance postcode is ME17 1RG.

Via the M20: Leave the M20 at Junction 8 (Lenham A20) and follow the brown and white tourist signs for Leeds Castle. At the third roundabout, turn right onto the B2163. The main entrance is a few hundred yards on the left.

Important for Sat Nav Users: Once you reach the A20, please follow the brown and white tourist signs to avoid being directed to a private entrance.

Staff at the main gate will direct you to the Castle car park. Transport is available for luggage or those with walking difficulties.

By Train & Taxi

From London: The nearest stations are Bearsted and Hollingbourne. Both are served by Southeastern trains from London Victoria (~75 mins) and Ashford International (~30 mins).

Taxi: From the station, a taxi to Leeds Castle takes approximately 5 minutes. Taxis can be ordered via local services or apps like Uber.
